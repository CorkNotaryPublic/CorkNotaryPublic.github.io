I was appointed as a Notary Public and Commissioned for Life by the Chief Justice of the Supreme Court in 2008 and have been working as a Notary Public in Cork City and County since then.

I am based in Cork City centre with flexible availability to come to your office or home or to meet you at a convenient location. Out of hours and weekend appointments are available. All appointments are by prior arrangement so if you would like to meet please contact me. I always provide a professional, friendly and efficient service at a competitive price.

Member of the Faculty of Notary Publics in Ireland.

Ideally, you should send me a scan or photo of all documentation that you require notarised in advance and upon seeing the instructions from the end user and seeing what requires to be notarised I can usually give you a firm quote.

There are often other costs incurred in addition to notarisation, such as having your documentation apostilled and legalised if required. This process can take some time at the Department of Foreign Affairs and any embassies that are involved. I can also arrange a courier to send your documentation to the end user, on request which will create further charges. Out of hours or travel can also affect the cost but not always.
